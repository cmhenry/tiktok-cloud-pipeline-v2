## LABELS

### Reportable Content
Content that violates safety and civility standards by containing harassment, hate speech, violence, or sexual exploitation.

#### Includes

- **Harassment and Bullying**: Insulting someone or threatening to insult someone, including using profanity or obscene language to degrade them; showing, promoting, or threatening harassment or bullying (physical or otherwise) of others, including coordinated harassment; doxing, blackmailing, revealing, or calling for the reveal of private or sensitive information

- **Hate Speech and Hateful Behaviors**: Encouraging violence, segregation, discrimination, or other harm towards people based on protected attributes (race, ethnicity, gender, religion, sexual orientation, disability, caste, immigration status, national origin, serious disease, sex, tribe); demeaning someone on the basis of these characteristics including dehumanizing people, portraying protected groups as inherently dangerous, using hateful slurs, deadnaming or misgendering, promoting conversion therapy; denying or minimizing well-documented historical events that harmed protected groups (Holocaust, Rwandan genocide); supporting or spreading hateful ideologies including white supremacy, misogyny, anti-LGBTQ+ hate, antisemitism, Islamophobia, or hateful conspiracies; displaying symbols and imagery associated with hateful movements

- **Physical Violence and Violent Threats**: Showing, promoting, or threatening physical violence including real-world torture, graphic violence, and extreme physical fighting; promoting or materially supporting violent or hateful organizations, including violent extremists and criminal organizations

- **Sexual Exploitation and Abuse**: Showing or promoting non-consensual sexual acts (real or fictional) including rape and molestation; showing or promoting non-consensual sharing of intimate content or threats to share such content; editing content to sexualize someone or create the appearance of them engaging in sexual activity; unwanted or degrading statements about someone's private body parts, sexual activity, or private sex life

#### Excludes

- **Unedited Media**: Unedited movie or TV clips
- **Satire and Commentary**: Content using offensive language in clearly satirical or comedic contexts without genuine harmful intent
- **Criticism of Public Figures**: Content criticizing people in positions of significant public power (politicians, police, public officials) focusing on their actions or policies rather than targeting protected attributes
- **In-group Usage**: Content using potentially offensive language where the speaker is a member of the same group being referenced
- **Educational Content**: Educational or documentary content with appropriate framing that does not promote harmful behavior
- **Out of Scope**: Content that may be problematic but does not fall into the four specified categories (harassment, hate speech, violence, sexual exploitation)
